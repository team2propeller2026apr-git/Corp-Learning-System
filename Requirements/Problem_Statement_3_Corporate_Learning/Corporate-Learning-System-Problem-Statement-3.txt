Corporate Learning Progress, Intervention & Compliance Tracking System
    a.	Problem Statement: Organizations are accountable for employee learning outcomes, training attendance, and compliance with regulatory bodies and industry standards. Yet learning data is fragmented across LMS platforms, assessment systems, and trainer notes. Employees who fall behind on required competencies are detected too late, and interventions are reactive. Build a digital learning progress and early-intervention tracking system that consolidates training attendance, assessment results, and competency milestones to identify at-risk learners and track intervention effectiveness.
    b.	Scope (You are not building a full corporate LMS):
        i.	Ingestion of (API):
            1.	Employee training attendance records
            2.	Periodic assessment scores
Competency-level learning milestones
    ii.	Rule-based risk identification (Define Rule definition format)
    iii.	Intervention tracking:
        1.	Remedial training sessions
        2.	Coaching and mentoring assignments
    iv.	Compliance-ready reporting
    c.	Must-Have Features
        i.	Employee learning profile aggregation
        ii.	Configurable risk rules (attendance %, score thresholds)
        iii.	At-risk learner classification
        iv.	Intervention history and outcome tracking
        v.	Simple dashboards or reports
    d.	Evaluation Parameters:
        i.	Realism of learning competency rules
        ii.	Correct handling of competency-level progression
        iii.	Practical usefulness for trainers and L&D administrators
        iv.	Clean separation of data, rules, and reporting
